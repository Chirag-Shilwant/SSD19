Workflow of Online Auction system

Different Classes in Online food ordering system

User:
User is a person who can be either a Seller who creates an auction of his/her product or a Bidder willing to participate in a bid. You have to drop attributes here.
Bidder:
A Bidder(a potential Buyer) bids on a product with a amount/value. You have to drop attributes here.
Auction:
Auction - A Bidder can bid on an Auction created by a Seller. Each auction will have it's unique ID. You have to drop attributes here.
Buyer:
A Buyer is a person who is willing to bid on a product.
Admin
Admin is an administrator who manages the entire Auction System Portal. You have to drop attributes here.
